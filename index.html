WHO ARE WE:

Dedicated to environmental conservation, we focused on reducing greenhouse gas emissions by tackling unnecessary food waste and minimizing the strain on public resources linked to the production and management of surplus food. Passionate wastage preventer dedicated to addressing food waste at closing time and helping merchants financially recover the manufacturing cost of leftover food.
Affordable meal provider intended to providing an opportunity for communities to enjoy affordable meals and allowing people to explore delicious cuisines at a low cost.

How We Reduce Food Waste?

In Australia, approximately 7.3 million tonnes of food is wasted each year. Our goal is to create a platform where merchants can sell surplus food items in term of surprise bag, which can be used to prepare a meal, at a discounted price. This ena- bles them to recover costs, while also giving others the opportunity to explore and enjoy new cuisines at a lower expense. Every surprise bag a customer receives saves roughly 2.5kg of CO2 emission.
